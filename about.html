About us

Even before there was a Little Ethiopia in addis Ababa : 4-kilo, it was our mission to bring true traditional Ethiopian Style cuisine to the residents and visitors of Our City and surrounding African cities.


Our dishes have always been crafted from authentic recipes, full of delicious flavor.

Explore Menu

When brothers Abebe and Ayele took over Ethio-Messob Restaurant, their vision was clear and simple: craft and serve the freshest and most flavorful Ethiopian cuisine that Angelinos could ask for. Three decades after Messob first opened it's doors in A.A, their commitment still stands and holds true. Today, Messob has grown into an institution in Little Ethiopia and has since been a favorite destination for the vegan community as well as foodies and tourists alike.
